About our store

We are a local record store located in downtown Boise, Idaho. As we realize the world has turned to online record sales, we decided to open up our music catalog to the internet and sell music online. While we offer an extensive collection of digital music, we still are open for business and have an amazing collection of vinyl collectors items to offer. So stop on by and check out what we have to offer.
